Audit Conclusion

Recommended deployment: Logistic Regression for contexts requiring full regulatory auditability (RBI FREE-AI "Understandable by Design"). Its coefficients constitute the explanation itself — no approximation risk. The Neural Network achieves marginally higher ROC-AUC but at the cost of SHAP stability (0.0023 vs 0.0000 for RF), making its explanations less reproducible under small input perturbations — a meaningful risk in employment decision contexts explicitly classified as high-risk under the EU AI Act.
